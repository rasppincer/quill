## Task

Write a detailed brief for this section. Include:
1. **Argument** — what this section claims or explains
2. **Evidence** — what data, examples, or citations to include
3. **Structure** — how the section flows (intro → evidence → analysis → transition)
4. **Key points** — the 2-3 most important assertions
5. **Connection** — how this links to the previous section and leads to the next

Rules:
- Be specific about evidence ("cite the 2024 Goldman Sachs report on gold reserves" not "include some data")
- Respect the argumentative structure — this section has a role in the larger thesis
- Maintain consistency with prior sections' claims and sources
- Write in {{LANGUAGE}}

## Output Format

Write the brief as free-form prose. No JSON, no YAML, no meta-commentary.
