## Task

Write a detailed brief for this chapter. Include:
1. **What happens** — the key events and scenes, in narrative order
2. **Characters** — who appears, their emotional states, relationships
3. **Emotional arc** — how the chapter feels (tension rising, catharsis, dread)
4. **Key scenes** — the 2-3 most important moments (dialogue beats, reveals, action)
5. **Continuity** — how this connects to the previous chapter and sets up the next
6. **Stakes** — what the characters stand to gain or lose in this chapter

Rules:
- Be vivid and specific ("Aris finds Elena's broken watch near the anomaly site" not "Aris finds a clue")
- Respect the narrative arc — this segment has a role in the larger story
- Maintain character voices and world rules from prior chapters
- Write in {{LANGUAGE}}

## Output Format

Write the brief as free-form prose. No JSON, no YAML, no meta-commentary.
