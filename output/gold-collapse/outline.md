# Outline: Златото на Заклинателя

## Structure Overview

Five-act structure with prologue and epilogue. 12 chapters. ~10,500 words.
Tight thriller pacing — short sentences, scene-driven, no filler. Hemingway influence: economy of language, subtext over exposition.

## Sections

### Prologue: Кървавият петък (Bloody Friday)
- **Purpose:** Hook. Drop the reader into the crisis. Gold is crashing. Daniel watches.
- **Key beats:** Gold price falling (4012→3900→3600). Discord guild message ("my gold disappeared from stash"). Daniel makes the connection — two puzzle pieces nobody else sees together.
- **Estimated length:** ~400 words
- **Pacing:** Fast. Urgent. Three monitors, cold coffee, unlit cigarette.

### Part 1: Играчът (The Player) — Chapters 1-3
- **Purpose:** Character establishment. Set up Daniel's unique position: finance background + hardcore PoE 2 player + Chinese stream follower.
- **Key beats:**
  - Ch.1: Daniel's background (UNSS finance, quit brokerage, trades alone). 12K hours in PoE. Guild "Exalted Minds" (15 people, 8 countries). Discovers GoldenFarmer888 on Bilibili. The "Transmutation Stone" video — gold nugget with "Can be extracted" text.
  - Ch.2: Daniel's journal. Six weeks of tracking Chinese PoE 2 channels vs gold futures. March 15 — Guangzhou factory video. March 22 — Shenzhen factory with white coats. March 29 — Reddit post (deleted in 2h). April 5 — GoldenFarmer888 deactivates, 3 streamers replace him. April 12 — Zhihu post about quantum-digital bridge technology. April 19 — Bloomberg can't explain the crash. Correlation is undeniable.
  - Ch.3: Maria. The ex-girlfriend. "You don't live, Daniel. You observe." Balcony, cigarette, orange sky over Mladost 4. Gold at 3200. He decides to act.
- **Estimated length:** ~3,000 words
- **Pacing:** Slow burn. Builds dread through accumulation of detail. Each journal entry tightens the screw.

### Part 2: Катастрофата (The Catastrophe) — Chapters 4-6
- **Purpose:** The crash happens. Daniel's theory confirmed. He takes positions.
- **Key beats:**
  - Ch.4: First week of May. Gold 3100→1400 in 5 days. Bloomberg/CNBC in frenzy. CNBC interviews retired teacher from Ohio — lost 40% of pension. Daniel watches, knows the cause, can't say it.
  - Ch.5: Friday evening. Daniel calls Maria. "I just wanted to hear your voice." Then: VPN → Weibo, Bilibili, Zhihu, Telegram. Pieces together the full picture: Shenzhen Quantum Materials Lab, Tencent integration, 100K Gold = 1 Nugget = 1 gram. Bot farms: 10K accounts, 24/7, 500g gold/day. $800M/year from a video game. Vodka.
  - Ch.6: Daniel trades. Short gold (CFD, 100oz @ 1400). Short silver (500oz @ 18). Long mining stocks (Barrick @ 12, Newmont @ 28, Agnico @ 34). Then: the agony of waiting. Nightmare — gold at zero, Maria says "I told you."
- **Estimated length:** ~3,000 words
- **Pacing:** Explosive. Ch.4 is chaos. Ch.5 is revelation. Ch.6 is tension.

### Part 3: Пробивът (The Breakthrough) — Chapters 7-9
- **Purpose:** Daniel acts. Goes from observer to participant. The truth goes public.
- **Key beats:**
  - Ch.7: Discord reveal. Daniel shares screen with Exalted Minds. ExaltSlayer drops the bomb: Deutsche Bank knows. Has known for 2 months. €2B short on gold. "If Deutsche Bank gets caught, it's Enron 2.0."
  - Ch.8: Nikolay Nikolov — Bulgarian businessman with LBMA connections. Comes to Mladost 4, sees everything. "How much can we make?" "That's not why I'm here." Deal: London meeting, Nikolov wants to know first when gold rebounds.
  - Ch.9: London. Richard Hughes, LBMA director. Hilton near Hyde Park. 40-minute presentation. "You're telling me gold is falling because someone farms it in a video game." Conference call with CME, COMEX, FCA, Chinese securities commission. Director Chen: "We know about the factories. The technology is government-owned." GoldenFarmer888 = Chen Wei, ex-Tencent engineer, whistle-blower. Technology works with ANY physical material.
- **Estimated length:** ~2,500 words
- **Pacing:** Rising. Each scene escalates the stakes. From Discord → Sofia apartment → London boardroom.

### Part 4: Разкритието (The Revelation) — Chapters 10-12
- **Purpose:** Resolution. Consequences. The world responds.
- **Key beats:**
  - Ch.10: June 3 — Chinese government shuts down Shenzhen Lab, seals 400+ factories, 2000+ arrests. SEC summons Daniel. Testifies via video call from Mladost 4. "How did you figure out what thousands of Wall Street analysts couldn't?" "Because thousands of analysts don't play PoE 2." Traded on public info — not insider trading. $347K net profit.
  - Ch.11: Gold rebounds. 1100→3600 in one week. Biggest single-day gain in history. Panic reverses.
  - Ch.12: Daniel checks positions. Shorts stopped out (-$20K gold, -$8K silver). Mining stocks: Barrick 12→38, Newmont 28→72, Agnico 34→89. Net: $347K. Balcony, cigarette, Mladost 4 unchanged.
- **Estimated length:** ~1,500 words
- **Pacing:** Fast resolution, then slow exhale.

### Epilogue: Златото се връща (Gold Returns)
- **Purpose:** Aftermath. Reflection. Bittersweet closure.
- **Key beats:** 6 months later. Gold at $4800 (higher than pre-crisis — irony). SEC cleared Daniel. Lawyer cost $15K (half the profit). Deutsche Bank made €800M. Nikolov made 2M BGN. Technology is alive — new lab, new name. GoldenFarmer888 (Chen Wei) is dead — all accounts erased. Guild still plays. Maria calls. "Was it you?" "Yes." "Do you want me to come?" "Yes." Cigarette, orange sky, dogs barking. Gold can wait. Maps can't. But Maria is coming. And that matters more than gold.
- **Estimated length:** ~1,100 words
- **Pacing:** Quiet. Reflective. The last line lands the emotional punch.

## Character Arcs

### Daniel Стоянов (Zaataru)
- **Start:** Passive observer. 12K hours in PoE. Trades alone. Lost Maria. "You don't live, you observe."
- **Turn:** Realizes he knows something nobody else knows. The observer becomes actor.
- **End:** Still in Mladost 4. Still smokes. But Maria is coming. He chose to act, and it mattered.

### Maria
- **Role:** Emotional anchor. The life Daniel could have.
- **Arc:** Left him (or screens). Returns in the epilogue. Her question — "Was it you?" — is the moral judgment the story needs.

### ExaltSlayer
- **Role:** Mirror. The insider who knew and stayed silent.
- **Arc:** Deutsche Bank trader who knew before Daniel. Chose profit over disclosure. Went to Thailand after.

### GoldenFarmer888 / Чен Уей
- **Role:** The whistle-blower. Catalyst.
- **Arc:** Ex-Tencent engineer. Streamed to show the world. Disappeared. Dead. The cost of truth.

### Николай Николов
- **Role:** Pragmatic facilitator. "How much can we make?"
- **Arc:** Represents the system's response — everything must be profitable to matter.

### Ричард Хюз (LBMA)
- **Role:** Authority figure. Skeptic turned believer.
- **Arc:** "This is the biggest market manipulation I've seen." → Acts decisively.

### Директор Чен
- **Role:** Chinese government representative.
- **Arc:** Knew about the factories. Technology is government-owned. The geopolitical layer.

## Themes

- **Observer → Participant:** Daniel watches screens his whole life. The story is about when watching isn't enough.
- **Digital → Physical:** A video game currency becomes real gold. The boundary dissolves.
- **Knowledge as burden:** Knowing the truth doesn't set you free — it traps you.
- **Hemingway principle:** The iceberg. What's unsaid matters more than what's said. Maria's silence. ExaltSlayer's guilt. Chen Wei's death.

## Notes

- Language: Bulgarian throughout. Dialogue feels natural, not translated.
- Title: "Златото на Заклинателя" (The Sorcerer's Gold) — double meaning: the Transmutation Stone is literally sorcery, and Daniel "casts a spell" on the market by revealing the truth.
