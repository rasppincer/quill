# Review Checklist

## Structure
- [ ] All outline beats addressed
- [ ] Sections flow logically
- [ ] No missing transitions between sections
- [ ] Opening hooks the reader
- [ ] Ending earns its weight (not rushed, not padded)

## Pacing
- [ ] No sections that drag (repeated info, over-description)
- [ ] No sections that rush (important beats glossed over)
- [ ] Tension rises/falls as intended
- [ ] Reader has room to breathe between intense sections

## Quality
- [ ] No repeated scenes or arguments
- [ ] Character/voice consistency throughout
- [ ] Dialogue sounds natural (not exposition dumps)
- [ ] Specific > vague (concrete details over abstractions)
- [ ] No padding (every paragraph earns its place)

## Language
- [ ] Grammar and spelling clean
- [ ] Sentence structure varied (not same rhythm throughout)
- [ ] Word choice precise (not thesaurus-y)
- [ ] No AI-isms (see humanizer checklist)
